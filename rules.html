Server Rules

Please read all rules carefully before playing on our server. Ignorance of rules is not an excuse.

01. Maximum Team Limit

The max team limit on this server is 3 players. Alliances, shared bases, or roaming together in groups larger than 3 is strictly prohibited and results in a ban.

02. Cheating & Exploits

Any usage of third-party software, hacks, macros, game file modifications, or exploiting unintendent game bugs leads to an immediate permanent ban.

03. Chat & Behavior

Harassment, hate speech, spamming chat, or excessive toxicity towards other players or staff members will result in mutes or bans.
